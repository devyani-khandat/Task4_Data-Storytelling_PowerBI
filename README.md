# 📊 Task 4: Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation

## 📌 Overview
This project is part of my **Data Analytics Internship at ApexPlanet**.
The objective of Task 4 is to transform data analysis into meaningful insights using **data storytelling and basic statistical validation**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Convert data analysis into a clear business story
* Identify key trends and patterns
* Apply basic statistical understanding
* Support decision-making with insights

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
* Power BI
* Microsoft PowerPoint

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights
* Total Sales: **2.30M**
* Total Profit: **286.40K**
* Total Discount: **1.56K**

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
* Technology is the top-performing category
* Furniture has low profitability despite decent sales
* West region generates the highest sales
* South region shows the lowest performance
* Profit margins vary across categories

---

## 📐 Statistical Validation
* Observed variation in profit distribution across categories
* High sales does not always result in high profit
* Negative relationship between discount and profit
* Higher discounts can reduce overall profitability

---

## 🧪 Hypothesis Testing
* **H0:** Discount has no significant effect on profit
* **H1:** Discount significantly affects profit
**Result:**
A negative relationship was observed between discount and profit.
**Conclusion:**
Optimizing discount strategies can help improve profitability.

---

## 📊 Deliverables
* Power BI Dashboard
* Presentation (PPT)
* Insights & Analysis

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
The analysis highlights that while sales performance is strong, profitability is uneven. Strategic focus is needed on improving profit margins and optimizing underperforming segments.
